How to get help?

Please follow the steps below:

  • Go to instructions on the menu above. This might already solve your issue.
  • Talk to other sites that are using the VR Hazard Tour App. Maybe they had a similar issue and solved it.
  • Talk to your regional HSE SPOC. She/he might be able to help.
  • If your issue perists, please post a summary on Yammer. Look for the group "Data Visualization and Machine Learning in HS&E" and post your questions there. The whole community can see your request and help.
  • If your issue still remains unsolved, please contact Christoph Wagner-Gillen (wagnergillen.c@pg.com). He has written the application and will be able to help you.
